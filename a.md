"This is the Crisis Correlation Network. Each circle is a financial institution; its colour is its cluster — a group the model found on its own, with no labels, purely from the correlations our quantum computer generated. And remarkably, those clusters track the real world — region and business type. The legend shows each cluster's purity: how cleanly it maps to a single region — high purity means the quantum-found correlations rediscovered real structure by themselves. The lines are correlations, split two ways in the legend: contagion — positive correlation, firms that fail together — and hedge — negative correlation, where one holding up cushions the other. The thickest lines are the highest-correlation pairs — the strongest transmission channels in the system. Drag the slider to keep only those — the contagion backbone — and click any institution for its failure odds and likely co-failures. So, we are going to be collapsing every single big bank and seeing how to correlate different kinds of financial institutions."
