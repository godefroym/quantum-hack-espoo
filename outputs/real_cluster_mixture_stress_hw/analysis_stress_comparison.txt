REAL 38-ENTITY HARDWARE -- STRESS vs BASELINE COMPARATIVE ANALYSIS
====================================================================
Backend: ibm_boston | 200,000 shots/cluster | same 14/14/10 partition
BASELINE-HW: real ~0.2% PDs, all 38 below the 2.7% noise floor.
STRESS-HW:   2008-calibrated ~15% PDs, all 38 ABOVE the floor (SNR mean ~5.6).
SIM-PREVIEW: exact statevector on the stressed spec = the faithful target.
Artifacts: analysis_stress_comparison.json (all numbers), .png, this file.

BOTTOM LINE
-----------
Lifting the signal above the noise floor turned the real network from UNMEASURABLE into a
PARTIAL, structurally-meaningful hardware demonstration -- but NOT a magnitude-faithful one.
What stress BOUGHT us: the marginal ORDERING moves from noise to a genuinely positive rank signal
(global Spearman vs target +0.25 baseline -> +0.36 stress), and the within-cluster |corr| recovery becomes
REAL rather than spurious (71% of target on above-floor targets vs the baseline's noise-manufactured
correlations on ~0 targets). The cross-cluster coupling is reconstructed (97% of reference) -- but that is the
classical reconciler, not the device.
What it did NOT buy: faithful marginal MAGNITUDES. Decoherence still pulls every qubit toward
0.5, so the overall marginal RMSE vs target stays large (0.187, mean signed
bias +0.150 = upward pull). The cascade tail is therefore HARDWARE-PERTURBED
(overstated), not ground truth.

VERDICT
-------
YES -- the stress regime makes the real 38-entity network a LEGITIMATE, if qualified, hardware
demonstration. Above the noise floor the QPU recovers a real (if uneven) marginal ORDERING and
GENUINELY retains within-cluster correlation (71% of target) instead of the baseline's noise-manufactured
spurious correlation -- and the cascade count law moves from TV 0.92 (no signal) to TV 0.44 vs truth.
This is a real signal, not decoherence -- the before/after vs the buried baseline is unambiguous.
CAVEATS: (1) marginal MAGNITUDES remain decoherence-biased upward toward 0.5 (RMSE ~0.19),
so absolute PDs are not trustworthy; (2) the cascade tail numbers are therefore HARDWARE-PERTURBED and
overstate risk relative to the faithful sim-preview; (3) the cross-cluster correlation is rebuilt by
the CLASSICAL reconciler (fit to the spec target), not measured on the device. Honest framing:
a correlation/ordering-faithful hardware demo, magnitude- and tail-perturbed.
